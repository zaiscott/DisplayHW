Visualizing Distributions

Distribution

Cairo uses the term, "Distribution" to refer to all of the values of a data set together which is typically shown by a graph. Other things that Cairo discussed in the chapter was the 4 elemts of a distribution that help descibe the data.

The Elements of Distribution

Level
Another term for the measures of central tendency, which help give an estamation on sizes of numbers and the center point.
Spread
Tells how wide the set of data is which helps indicate the range, stadard deviation, quatiles, and variances.
Shape
Indicates the spread of the data, where the mean lies, its variability, and possibly what the range of the data set is.
Outliers
is a data point that differs significantly from other data points, which can be a result of experimental error. Outliers can cause problems in situations of analysis of the data.
